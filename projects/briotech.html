automated manufacturing optimization using AI

Description

Briotech is a world-class manufacturer of hypochlorous acid (HOCl) 
and a pioneer of innovation for the creation of the world’s first geographically dispersed HOCl manufacturing networks, BrioWHISH® Systems (WHISH).

I worked on the WHISH for 7 months to develop an AI that predicts their product outcome based on the machine's parameters.

    I can't reveal full details of the proprietary deatils due to an NDA, but disclosure of my contribution without disclosing proprietary information has been permitted by the company.
